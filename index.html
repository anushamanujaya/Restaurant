Our Story

Established in 2010, Bistro Noir has been a cornerstone of fine dining excellence in the heart of New York City. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a dining experience that transcends the ordinary and elevates every meal into a memorable occasion.

Our passionate team of chefs, led by Executive Chef Marcus Laurent, brings together decades of culinary expertise from renowned kitchens around the world. We source the finest ingredients from local farms and trusted suppliers, ensuring that every dish reflects our commitment to quality and sustainability.

At Bistro Noir, we believe that dining is more than just nourishment—it's an art form. From our carefully curated wine selection to our attentive service, every detail is designed to create an atmosphere of sophistication and warmth that makes every visit special.
